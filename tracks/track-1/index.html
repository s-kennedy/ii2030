Precision Agriculture for Smallholder Farmers

How can we increase smallholder yields through precision agriculture?

What is disruptive technology doing for agriculture?

Precision Agriculture uses sensors, Big Data and digitized farming equipment to enable farmers to observe, measure and respond to variabilities in their environment (e.g. climate, soil composition, crop practices and disease). With this technology, farmers around the world are able to conserve valuable resources, such as water and fertilizer, while increasing both quality and quantity of their yields.

Day 1: Inclusive Innovation Tour

Hit the road on our Inclusive Innovation Tour of Berlin!

Our tour guides will take participants on an exploration of Berlin’s buzzing inclusive innovation scene to provide inspiration and information for each track.

The 4-hour tour will take participants to 3 visionary organizations, where hosts will lead a discussion on how greater inclusion in the agriculture sector will impact society in 2030
