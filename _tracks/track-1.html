Day 1: Inclusive Innovation Tour

Hit the road on our Inclusive Innovation Tour of Berlin!

Our tour guides will take participants on an exploration of Berlin’s buzzing inclusive innovation scene to provide inspiration and information for each track.

The 4-hour tour will take participants to 3 visionary organizations, where hosts will lead a discussion on how greater inclusion in the agriculture sector will impact society in 2030

The day will end with a “Meet & Greet” networking dinner. Participants from all tracks will reconvene over dinner to share what they have learned and get to know one another.
