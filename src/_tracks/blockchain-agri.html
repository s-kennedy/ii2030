It makes transactions recordable and trackable, providing a system that is transparent, efficient and market-oriented. Blockchain ensures the integrity of the value exchanged without having a trusted third party. For more information www.thefork.online - question: Why will it impact sustainable consumption and production processes? answer: Per capita, we are eating more and we are eating better. We know more about our food. And we demand more of our food. This gives an increasingly complex food system with higher levels of risk, serious transportation and traceability challenges, and disturbing waste and recall costs, to name just a few implications.

Blockchain signposts a new era of openness, decentralization and global inclusion. The technology can accelerate solutions for the issues arising in complex food systems, enhancing – and even disrupting - sustainable consumption and production.

However, to what extent it will accelerate and disrupt results on SDGs, depends on further technical developments and – notoriously - its implementation. Will blockchain make current consumption and production processes more sustainable, will it create whole new governance structures, or is it just another hype, will we work as we do now, just using different names? - question: Who would we like to attend? answer: ii2030 aims to bring together experts and organizations who will contribute to the solution – and continue its development and implementation following the 2-day event.
