Conference Overview

ii2030 will bring together innovators from
corporates
to create solutions for a more inclusive society by 2030

Over two exciting days, participants from every sector will connect, collaborate and construct new ideas.

Agenda

Inclusive Innovation Tour

Oct 18, 2017 — All around Berlin

Several tour guides will take participants on an exploration of Berlin’s buzzing inclusive innovation scene to provide inspiration and information around the ii2030 challenges.

Inclusive Innovation Factory

Oct 19, 2017 — Neue Mälzerei, Berlin

In design-thinking labs of up to 10 participants, we will work out how technology can drive inclusive innovation, and what is needed to realize solutions. Participants are carefully selected to bring together those actors who can make the change - and only those.
