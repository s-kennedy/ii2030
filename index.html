Conference Overview

ii2030 will bring together innovators from corporates
to create solutions for a more inclusive society by 2030 ii2030 will bring together innovators from corporates, startups, the public sector, NGOs, and science to create solutions for a more inclusive society by 2030

Over two exciting days, participants from every sector will connect, collaborate and construct new ideas.

The conference will foster mutual learning, as well as the creation of valuable partnerships to enhance and share knowledge.

ii2030 will be a first step in exploring not only why these challenges to inclusion exist, but also how we can apply cutting-edge technology and solutions to interrupt them and build a more inclusive society by 2030.
